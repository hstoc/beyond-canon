Homestuck2: Beyond Canon Table of Contents

This is a fan-made table of contents for Homestuck2: Beyond Canon (HSBC).

HSBC was written in two phases. From 2019 to 2020, the original HSBC team wrote the first 17 chapters. In 2023, a new team began writing the story.

For an overview and history of HSBC, see this MSPA Wiki page. For HSBC credits, see the HSBC site's credits page.

2023–Ongoing

HSBC chapters written by the new team. This division into chapters, and their titles, are unofficial and chosen by me.

I've divided the chapters where it feels like a new section has started, which is usually 10–25 pages. Most chapters correspond exactly to one update (that is, the chapter was posted all on one day, with nothing else). A few chapters were posted together on one day, and a couple were posted across multiple days.

Each title is a quote from the chapter itself. I try to choose something memorable or iconic in its own right, and something thematic to the whole chapter.
