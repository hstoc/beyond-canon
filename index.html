This is a fan-made table of contents for Homestuck2: Beyond Canon (HSBC).

HSBC was written in two phases. From 2019 to 2020, the original HSBC team wrote the first 17 chapters. In 2023, a new team began writing the story.

For an overview and history of HSBC, see this MSPA Wiki page. For HSBC credits, see the HSBC credits.
