Homestuck2: Beyond Canon
Content Calendar

This is a fan-made calendar showing all HSBC content that has been released since the relaunch in Oct 2023.

Each table is one month. Each day shows icons representing the content released that day:

  • HSBC updates
    • Meat
    • Candy
    • Helltier
  • Bonus art
    • Illustration
    • Bonus comic
    • Music track preview
  • Commentary
    • Writer commentary
    • Artist commentary
  • Other
    • News post
The Meat icon is adapted from The Homestuck Epilogues. The Candy icon is adapted from the Twitter/X candy emoji. The Helltier icon is adapted from HSBC itself.

Calendar layout and HSBC schedule theories

By experimenting with different ways of arranging each month, I've found that some patterns become clearest if you count by Saturdays. For example, for the last four months, the bonus comics have released precisely on the day before the 3rd and 4th Saturdays of the month. (Note that this pattern weakens if you count by Fridays instead, because that's sometimes the 2nd and 3rd Fridays, and sometimes the 3rd and 4th Fridays.) Similarly, there is typically bonus content on the day before the 2nd Saturday, and bonus content released on or before the 1st Saturday.

As for HSBC updates themselves, in August and September, they were released on the 2nd and 4th Saturdays or the next day. May through July kind of follow this pattern, but actually fit much better to the 2nd and 4th Wednesday. I'm not sure if they switched from a Wednesday schedule to a Saturday one, or if one or both patterns are coincidences.

Admittedly the data is shaky, because there have only been about 5 months of content released on a weekly basis rather than a monthly basis.
